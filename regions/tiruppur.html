திருப்பூர் மாவட்டம் பல்லடம் அருகே உள்ள கேத்தனூர் பகுதியில் இன்று காலை பள்ளி மாணவ, மாணவிகளை ஏற்றிச் சென்ற தனியார் பள்ளி வேன் ஒன்று எதிர்பாராதவிதமாக கட்டுப்பாட்டை இழந்து தலைகீழாக கவிழ்ந்து விபத்துக்குள்ளானது. இந்த கோர விபத்தில் வேனில் பயணம் செய்த தாரணி என்ற ஒன்பதாம் வகுப்பு மாணவி சம்பவ இடத்திலேயே பரிதாபமாக உயிரிழந்தார். மேலும் இந்த விபத்தில் படுகாயமடைந்த மற்ற 10-க்கும் மேற்பட்ட மாணவ, மாணவிகள் மற்றும் வேன் ஓட்டுநர் ஆகியோர் உடனடியாக மீட்கப்பட்டு பல்லடம் அரசு மருத்துவமனைக்கு சிகிச்சைக்காக அனுப்பி வைக்கப்பட்டனர். விபத்து குறித்து தகவல் அறிந்ததும் சம்பவ இடத்திற்கு விரைந்து வந்த அவசர கால மீட்புக்குழுவினர் மற்றும் காவல்துறையினர் மீட்புப் பணிகளை முடுக்கிவிட்டனர்.

இக்கோர விபத்துக்கான முதற்கட்ட விசாரணையில், பள்ளி வாகனத்தின் ஓட்டுநர் அதிவேகமாகவும் அஜாக்கிரதையாகவும் வண்டியை இயக்கியதே முக்கிய காரணம் என்று தெரியவந்துள்ளது. கேத்தனூர் சாலையின் வளைவுப் பகுதியில் திரும்பும்போது, வேகத்தைக் கட்டுப்படுத்த முடியாமல் ஓட்டுநரின் கட்டுப்பாட்டை இழந்த வேன் சாலையோரப் பள்ளத்தில் கவிழ்ந்துள்ளது. மேலும், விபத்துக்குள்ளான அந்த தனியார் பள்ளி வாகனம் தகுந்த பராமரிப்பு இன்றி இயக்கப்பட்டதா மற்றும் அதற்குரிய முறையான தகுதிச் சான்றிதழ் (FC) பெறப்பட்டுள்ளதா என்பது குறித்தும் வட்டாரப் போக்குவரத்து அலுவலக அதிகாரிகள் தீவிரமாக விசாரித்து வருகின்றனர். பள்ளி நிர்வாகத்தின் அலட்சியப் போக்கே இந்த இளம் பிஞ்சு உயிரிழப்பிற்கு வழிவகுத்துள்ளது என்று அப்பகுதி பொதுமக்கள் குற்றம் சாட்டுகின்றனர்.

இந்த விபத்து திருப்பூர் மாவட்ட மக்களிடையே பெரும் அதிர்ச்சியையும், சோகத்தையும் ஏற்படுத்தியுள்ளது. குறிப்பாக, பள்ளிக்குத் தங்கள் குழந்தைகளை அனுப்பிவிட்டு தங்களின் அன்றாட பணிகளுக்குச் சென்ற பெற்றோர்கள், இந்த விபத்துச் செய்தியைக் கேட்டு பதறியடித்துக்கொண்டு சம்பவ இடத்திற்கும் மருத்துவமனைக்கும் ஓடி வந்தனர். உயிரிழந்த மாணவியின் பெற்றோரின் கதறல் சத்தம் பார்ப்போரின் நெஞ்சைக் உலுக்குவதாக இருந்தது. பள்ளி வாகனங்களின் பாதுகாப்பற்ற பயணங்கள் மற்றும் ஓட்டுநர்களின் அஜாக்கிரதை குறித்து திருப்பூர் மாவட்ட பொதுமக்கள் மற்றும் சமூக ஆர்வலர்கள் தங்களது கடுமையான கண்டனங்களையும் கவலைகளையும் பதிவு செய்து வருகின்றனர்.

விபத்து குறித்து வழக்குப் பதிவு செய்துள்ள காமநாயக்கன்பாளையம் காவல் துறையினர், தப்பியோட முயன்ற பள்ளி வேன் ஓட்டுநரை உடனடியாகக் கைது செய்து விசாரணைப் வளையத்திற்குள் கொண்டு வந்துள்ளனர். இந்த விபத்து நடந்த இடத்தை நேரில் பார்வையிட்ட திருப்பூர் மாவட்ட காவல் கண்காணிப்பாளர் மற்றும் வருவாய்த் துறை அதிகாரிகள், விபத்துக்கான காரணங்கள் குறித்து விரிவான விசாரணை நடத்த உத்தரவிட்டுள்ளனர். மேலும், காயமடைந்து பல்லடம் மற்றும் திருப்பூர் அரசு மருத்துவமனைகளில் சிகிச்சை பெற்று வரும் குழந்தைகளுக்கு உயர்தர சிகிச்சை அளிக்கப்படுவதை உறுதி செய்யுமாறு மருத்துவக் குழுவினருக்கு மாவட்ட நிர்வாகம் அறிவுறுத்தியுள்ளது.

இனிவரும் காலங்களில் இதுபோன்ற துயர சம்பவங்கள் திருப்பூர் மாவட்டத்தில் மீண்டும் நிகழாமல் இருக்க அதிகாரிகள் கடுமையான நடவடிக்கைகளை அறிவித்துள்ளனர். அதன்படி, திருப்பூர் மாவட்டம் முழுவதும் உள்ள அனைத்து தனியார் பள்ளி வாகனங்களையும் உடனடியாகத் தணிக்கை செய்ய வட்டாரப் போக்குவரத்து அதிகாரிகளுக்கு (RTO) உத்தரவிடப்பட்டுள்ளது. தகுதிச் சான்றிதழ் இல்லாத மற்றும் விதிகளை மீறி இயக்கப்படும் பள்ளி வாகனங்களின் உரிமங்கள் உடனடியாக ரத்து செய்யப்படும் என்றும், ஓட்டுநர்களுக்கு முறையான பயிற்சி மற்றும் மருத்துவப் பரிசோதனைகள் கட்டாயமாக்கப்படும் என்றும் மாவட்ட ஆட்சியர் எச்சரித்துள்ளார். பள்ளி நிர்வாகங்கள் தங்கள் வாகனங்களின் பாதுகாப்பை உறுதி செய்யத் தவறினால் அவர்கள் மீது குற்றவியல் நடவடிக்கை பாயும் என்றும் அதிகாரிகள் தரப்பில் எச்சரிக்கை விடுக்கப்பட்டுள்ளது.

In a deeply tragic incident that has sent shockwaves through the local community, a young schoolgirl lost her life after a private van carrying school children overturned in Tiruppur district. The fatal accident occurred during the morning hours when the vehicle was transporting students to their respective educational institutions. According to local police sources, the driver of the van lost control of the vehicle while navigating a sharp bend, causing it to veer off the road and capsize. Emergency services and local residents rushed to the spot immediately to rescue the trapped children from the mangled remains of the vehicle.

The victim has been identified as a young female student residing within the Tiruppur limits, whose untimely demise has plunged her family and school peers into deep mourning. Several other students who were traveling in the same van sustained varying degrees of injuries and were immediately rushed to the nearest government hospital for urgent medical attention. Preliminary investigations suggest that the accident was primarily caused by reckless driving and speeding on a narrow stretch of road. Eyewitnesses reported that the van was operating at a high speed, leaving the driver with very little time to react when the vehicle began to wobble.

The tragic incident has sparked widespread outrage and concern among the residents of Tiruppur, who have long been complaining about the unsafe transit conditions for school children. Parents and local activists have pointed out that many private school vans and autorickshaws routinely flout safety norms, overloading vehicles far beyond their permitted capacity. Residents are now demanding stricter regulations and regular fitness checks for all vehicles deployed for transporting school children in the district. The local community has gathered to express their condolences to the bereaved family while calling for immediate systemic changes to prevent such heartbreaking occurrences in the future.

In response to the accident, senior district administration and police officials visited the spot and the hospital to monitor the medical treatment being provided to the injured students. The Tiruppur district police have registered a formal case against the driver of the private van under relevant sections of the law, and a comprehensive investigation has been launched to determine the exact sequence of events. Officials have assured the public that stringent action will be taken against the driver and the vehicle operator if any safety violations or negligence are proven during the probe.

Furthermore, the regional transport authorities in Tiruppur have announced a special drive to inspect all school-affiliated vehicles across the district starting this week. Educational institutions have been strictly warned to ensure that the transport operators they employ adhere strictly to the Supreme Court guidelines on school student safety. The district administration has also promised to conduct road safety awareness campaigns and install better signages at accident-prone zones to ensure smoother and safer transit for everyone.

The industrial hub of Tiruppur witnessed a wave of spiritual fervor as the highly anticipated consecration festival, known locally as the 'Kumbabishekam', commenced with grand rituals at the historic Sri Mahaliyamman Temple. Located in the heart of Tiruppur district, this ancient shrine has long been a spiritual sanctuary for thousands of local residents and textile workers who frequent the temple to seek divine blessings. The multi-day festival began with highly structured Vedic rituals, including the 'Ganapathi Homam' and 'Yagasala Pujas', performed by a group of revered chief priests who chanted sacred hymns to invoke peace and prosperity for the entire industrial region.

The primary objective behind this massive religious event is the spiritual rejuvenation of the temple structure, which recently underwent extensive renovation works funded by local philanthropists and the state administration. According to temple committee officials, such consecration ceremonies are traditionally performed once every twelve years to restore and amplify the spiritual energy of the deities housed within the sanctum sanctorum. The local administration, in coordination with the Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments (HR&CE) department, has made elaborate arrangements to ensure that the sacred water, which will be poured atop the temple towers during the main event, is prepared strictly in accordance with ancient Agamic scriptures.

The grand inaugural ceremonies attracted a massive gathering of devotees, local community leaders, and prominent administrative officials from across the Tiruppur district. Among the key dignitaries present were members of the temple trust, local civic body representatives, and senior police officials who arrived to review the security protocols. The organizers have expressed their deep gratitude to the local community for their overwhelming support, noting that the successful commencement of the festival is a testament to the deep-rooted cultural heritage and unity of the people of Tiruppur.

For the residents of Tiruppur, who are largely employed in the bustling garment and textile sectors, this festival serves as a major moment of communal harmony and spiritual relief from their demanding work schedules. Local business owners and residents have actively participated by organizing free food distribution camps, known as 'Annadanam', and establishing drinking water stations along the arterial roads leading to the temple. The local neighborhood associations have expressed immense joy over the scale of the celebrations, noting that the revitalized temple will continue to serve as a beacon of hope and cultural identity for future generations in the district.

In response to the massive influx of devotees, the Tiruppur district administration and the local police department have deployed a robust security apparatus to manage the crowd and prevent any untoward incidents. Official statements from the municipal corporation confirm that special transit routes, dedicated parking zones, and temporary medical aid posts have been established in the vicinity of the temple to ensure a hassle-free experience for the public. Senior administrative officials have assured the public that round-the-clock monitoring through CCTV cameras and dedicated sanitation drives will be maintained throughout the duration of the festival to guarantee the safety and well-being of all attendees.

The ambitious 'Plastic-Free Tiruppur' campaign, launched with much fanfare by the local administration, has unfortunately remained largely on paper, leaving residents and environmentalists deeply concerned over the grim reality on the ground. Despite official declarations and high-profile awareness drives aimed at eradicating single-use plastics across the textile hub, a walk through any major commercial street or residential area reveals a starkly different story. Thin plastic carry bags, banned non-biodegradable packaging materials, and disposable cups continue to be distributed openly by roadside vendors, daily markets, and even established retail outlets. The lack of sustained monitoring and a failure to enforce the ban strictly at the grassroots level have allowed the illegal plastic trade to thrive virtually unchecked throughout the city.

The primary reason behind this systemic failure is the sheer lack of affordable and accessible alternatives for small-scale merchants, coupled with a lacklustre enforcement drive by the municipal corporation. While large supermarkets have transitioned to cloth or paper bags, thousands of small tea stalls, flower vendors, and street food joints in Tiruppur rely heavily on cheap plastic bags to sustain their low-margin businesses. Local traders express frustration that while they are occasionally penalised during sporadic raids, the authorities have done very little to shut down the manufacturing units and wholesale distributors supplying these banned plastics to the district. Consequently, the ban has merely become a periodic annoyance for small businesses rather than a comprehensive solution to the mounting ecological crisis.

For the residents of Tiruppur, the consequences of this half-hearted implementation are becoming increasingly severe and hazardous to daily life. Discarded plastic waste now routinely chokes the city’s drainage systems, leading to severe waterlogging and artificial flooding even during moderate spells of rain. Furthermore, these clogged drains have become breeding grounds for disease-carrying mosquitoes, leading to a rise in vector-borne illnesses in densely populated residential quarters. The Noyyal River, which flows through the heart of the district, has also borne the brunt of this pollution, with massive piles of plastic debris accumulating along its banks and toxic microplastics leaching into the local soil and groundwater reserves.

In response to growing public criticism and the visible deterioration of the urban environment, senior officials from the Tiruppur City Municipal Corporation have defended their efforts while acknowledging the need for more stringent measures. Municipal authorities claim they have conducted numerous awareness campaigns in schools and colleges, distributed cloth bags in select wards, and levied fines on violating commercial establishments. However, officials also admit that their sanitary inspection teams are severely understaffed, making daily monitoring of thousands of shops across all zones an impossible task. The administration has now promised to form dedicated flying squads to conduct surprise inspections and seal shops that repeatedly violate the plastic ban, though skeptical residents remain doubtful of these promises.

To truly transform Tiruppur into a plastic-free zone, environmental activists argue that the administration must move beyond mere paper announcements and adopt a holistic, community-driven approach. There is an urgent need for the government to subsidise the production of eco-friendly alternatives like palm leaf containers, cloth bags, and compostable packaging to make them financially viable for small vendors. Additionally, waste segregation at source must be strictly enforced, and public recycling facilities must be upgraded to handle the existing plastic load. Until the municipal corporation bridges the gap between official policy and ground-level execution, the vision of a clean, green, and plastic-free Tiruppur will remain an elusive dream.

In a tragic incident that has sent shockwaves across the industrial hub of Tiruppur, a 28-year-old man was arrested by the local police for allegedly murdering his 22-year-old wife, who was four months pregnant. The gruesome crime took place in their rented residence located in the heavily populated neighborhood of Nallur within the Tiruppur corporation limits. According to police sources, the victim was identified as Nandhini, while her husband was identified as Karthik, an industrial worker employed in one of the local garment exporting units. The discovery of the young woman's lifeless body has cast a grim shadow over the local community, raising serious concerns regarding domestic safety and the rising instances of spousal violence in the district.

Preliminary investigations conducted by the Tiruppur North police division revealed that the couple had been married for just over a year and were expecting their first child together. However, their domestic life was reportedly marred by frequent altercations, primarily driven by suspicion and financial distress. On the night of the incident, a heated argument broke out between Karthik and Nandhini regarding a domestic dispute, which quickly escalated into physical violence. In a fit of rage, Karthik allegedly assaulted his pregnant wife brutally and strangled her to death before fleeing the spot, leaving her body on the floor of their house.

The tragic event came to light the following morning when neighbors noticed an unusual silence emanating from the house and decided to investigate further. Upon discovering Nandhini lying unresponsive, they immediately alerted the local police and emergency medical services, who rushed to the scene only to declare her dead on arrival. The body of the deceased woman was subsequently shifted to the Tiruppur Government Hospital for a comprehensive post-mortem examination to determine the exact cause of death. The news of a pregnant woman being murdered by her own husband spread rapidly through the residential colony, drawing a large crowd of shocked onlookers and grieving relatives to the spot.

The brutal murder has deeply disturbed the residents of Tiruppur, with community leaders and women's rights activists expressing deep outrage over the vulnerability of women within their domestic spaces. Neighbors described Nandhini as a quiet, gentle woman who was eagerly looking forward to motherhood, making the nature of her death even more heartbreaking for the local populace. Many residents in the locality have called for stricter vigilance and better community-level support systems to address domestic abuse before it escalates to such fatal levels. The incident has also triggered intense discussions among the working-class families of the textile city regarding the psychological toll of financial stress and the urgent need for counseling services.

Responding swiftly to the heinous crime, the Tiruppur police formed special teams to track down the accused, who had gone into hiding shortly after committing the murder. Following a coordinated search operation utilizing local intelligence and mobile tracking, Karthik was apprehended by the police near the city outskirts within a few hours of the crime being reported. A senior police official confirmed that a case has been registered under Section 302 (murder) of the Indian Penal Code, and the accused has been remanded to judicial custody after being produced before a local magistrate. The district administration and the police department have assured the public that a thorough investigation will be conducted to ensure that justice is served swiftly for the deceased woman and her unborn child.
