In a shocking incident that has sent ripples through the Salem district, a 21-year-old mother has been arrested for allegedly poisoning her 1.5-year-old son to death, revealing a complex tale of domestic discord and personal turmoil. The incident, which occurred in the Vagaroor South Forest area near Thalavasal, exposes the dark undercurrents of a troubled marital relationship that ultimately led to a tragic infanticide.

Lalitha, the accused, had been married to Thirumalai Vasudeven for six years, with their marriage characterized by frequent conflicts and eventual separation. The couple had two children - Harshan Raj, aged 5, and Puvan Raj, aged 1.5 - and had been living apart due to persistent disagreements. Lalitha had been residing at her parental home in Siruvachchur, while her husband was raising their elder son, highlighting the deep-seated familial tensions that preceded the heinous crime.

According to local police investigations, Lalitha had allegedly developed a relationship with a construction worker, which became a significant source of personal conflict. Sources suggest that she viewed her younger son, Puvan Raj, as an impediment to her personal happiness and extramarital relationship. In a premeditated act of desperation, she reportedly administered poison to the child in April, subsequently taking him to Aathur Government Hospital and claiming he had suffered seizures.

Medical professionals at the hospital became suspicious of the child's death and conducted thorough medical examinations. Forensic analysis confirmed that the child had indeed been poisoned, prompting immediate police intervention. The investigating team, led by Crime Branch Investigator Poornima, launched a comprehensive investigation that ultimately resulted in Lalitha's arrest two months after the incident. The police have registered a formal case and are conducting detailed interrogations to understand the complete sequence of events.

The tragic incident has deeply unsettled the local community in the Salem district, raising serious questions about domestic violence, marital discord, and the potential psychological pressures faced by individuals in complex family situations. Local authorities have emphasized the need for stronger support systems and counseling mechanisms to prevent such extreme measures. The case has also prompted discussions among social workers and law enforcement about identifying and supporting vulnerable family units before they reach such catastrophic breaking points. As the investigation continues, the community remains shocked and grieving, hoping for justice for the innocent child who became a victim of adult complexities.
