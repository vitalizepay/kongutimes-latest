சேலம் மாவட்டம் மகுடஞ்சாவடி ஒன்றியத்திற்குட்பட்ட தப்பகுட்டை ஊராட்சி பகுதியில், கடந்த சில வாரங்களாகவே கடுமையான குடிநீர் தட்டுப்பாடு நிலவி வருகிறது. இதனால் ஆத்திரமடைந்த அப்பகுதி பொதுமக்கள், தங்களுக்கு முறையான குடிநீர் விநியோகம் செய்ய வலியுறுத்தி, சேலம் - சங்ககிரி பிரதான நெடுஞ்சாலையில் திடீர் சாலை மறியல் போராட்டத்தில் ஈடுபட்டனர். காலி குடங்களுடன் சாலையில் அமர்ந்து பொதுமக்கள் போராட்டத்தில் ஈடுபட்டதால், அப்பகுதியில் போக்குவரத்து முற்றிலுமாக பாதிக்கப்பட்டது. குறிப்பாக, பள்ளி மற்றும் கல்லூரிகளுக்குச் செல்லும் மாணவ, மாணவிகளும், வேலைக்குச் செல்வோரும் கடும் அவதிக்குள்ளாகினர்.

இந்த குடிநீர் தட்டுப்பாட்டிற்கு முக்கிய காரணம், அப்பகுதியில் உள்ள ஆழ்துளை கிணறுகள் வறண்டு போனதும், காவிரி கூட்டுக்குடிநீர் திட்டத்தின் மூலம் வழங்கப்படும் குடிநீர் அளவு கணிசமாகக் குறைந்ததுமே ஆகும். தப்பகுட்டை ஊராட்சியில் உள்ள பல வார்டுகளுக்கு கடந்த இருபது நாட்களுக்கும் மேலாக ஒரு சொட்டு குடிநீர் கூட வழங்கப்படவில்லை என பொதுமக்கள் குற்றம் சாட்டுகின்றனர். இது குறித்து ஊராட்சி நிர்வாகத்திடம் பலமுறை மனு அளித்தும், எந்தவிதமான ஆக்கப்பூர்வமான நடவடிக்கையும் எடுக்கப்படாததால், வேறு வழியின்றி பொதுமக்கள் இந்த சாலை மறியல் போராட்டத்தை கையில் எடுத்ததாகத் தெரிவித்தனர்.

இந்தப் போராட்டத்தில் தப்பகுட்டை மற்றும் அதன் சுற்றுவட்டார கிராமங்களைச் சேர்ந்த நூற்றுக்கும் மேற்பட்ட பெண்கள் மற்றும் ஆண்கள் திரளாகப் பங்கேற்றனர். குறிப்பாக, இப்பகுதி பெண்களுக்கு அன்றாட வீட்டு வேலைகளைச் செய்வதற்கும், சமையல் செய்வதற்கும் கூட தண்ணீர் இல்லாத அவல நிலை ஏற்பட்டுள்ளது. தனியார் லாரிகளில் அதிக விலை கொடுத்து தண்ணீர் வாங்கும் நிலைக்கு தள்ளப்பட்டுள்ளதாக அவர்கள் வேதனை தெரிவித்தனர். ஏழை எளிய மக்கள் வசிக்கும் இப்பகுதியில், லாரி தண்ணீருக்கு தினமும் பணம் செலவழிப்பது தங்களின் வாழ்வாதாரத்தை பெரிதும் பாதிப்பதாக போராட்டத்தில் ஈடுபட்ட மக்கள் தெரிவித்தனர்.

போராட்டம் குறித்த தகவல் அறிந்ததும், மகுடஞ்சாவடி காவல் துறையினரும், ஒன்றிய வட்டார வளர்ச்சி அதிகாரிகளும் சம்பவ இடத்திற்கு விரைந்து வந்தனர். போராட்டத்தில் ஈடுபட்ட பொதுமக்களுடன் அவர்கள் சமரசப் பேச்சுவார்த்தை நடத்தினர். அப்போது, தப்பகுட்டை ஊராட்சிப் பகுதிகளில் நிலவும் குடிநீர் பிரச்னைக்கு தற்காலிகமாக லாரிகள் மூலம் உடனடியாக குடிநீர் விநியோகம் செய்ய ஏற்பாடு செய்யப்படும் என்றும், பழுதடைந்துள்ள ஆழ்துளை கிணறுகளை பழுதுநீக்கி, காவிரி கூட்டுக்குடிநீர் தடையின்றி கிடைக்க நிரந்தர தீர்வு காண நடவடிக்கை எடுக்கப்படும் என்றும் அதிகாரிகள் உறுதியளித்தனர்.

The residents of several residential areas in and around Salem city are facing severe hardships due to the sudden and prolonged disruption of drinking water supply, sparking widespread public outrage. For the past several days, major neighborhoods under the Salem Municipal Corporation, as well as adjoining suburban localities, have not received their designated quota of potable water. This acute shortage has forced families, particularly from lower and middle-income groups, to rely heavily on expensive private water tankers to meet their daily domestic needs. Local community activists report that the crisis has disrupted the routine of thousands of households, with many residents spending their early morning hours searching for alternative water sources or waiting in long queues at public community taps that yield only a trickle.

The primary cause behind this severe water scarcity is a major technical snag and pipeline burst along the dedicated transmission line of the Mettur-Salem Dedicated Water Supply Scheme. According to engineering sources within the civic body, a high-pressure distribution main suffered a significant rupture near the treatment plant, halting the pumping process entirely. Additionally, ongoing road widening works and underground sewerage pipeline installations in various parts of Salem have reportedly damaged auxiliary distribution lines, further compounding the distribution woes. Because these interconnected networks feed the overhead service reservoirs across multiple municipal wards, the localized pipeline damages have created a cascading effect, leaving several overhead tanks completely dry and unable to supply their designated zones.

In response to the growing public anger and sporadic road blockades staged by exasperated residents in some affected wards, senior officials of the Salem Municipal Corporation, led by the Municipal Commissioner and chief engineers, visited the key transit sites to inspect the ongoing repair works. The administration has deployed emergency maintenance crews to work round-the-clock to patch the ruptured main pipeline and restore regular pumping from the Mettur dam source. Officials have publicly appealed to the citizens of Salem for patience, assuring them that technical teams are putting in maximum effort to resolve the issue. The civic body has also promised to temporarily deploy municipal water tankers to the worst-hit neighborhoods to mitigate the immediate crisis until the piped supply is fully restored.

The impact of this water disruption has been particularly severe on local businesses, small eateries, and educational institutions operating within the Salem municipal limits. Many resident welfare associations have expressed deep disappointment over the lack of prior communication from the civic body regarding the supply shutdown, which prevented them from storing adequate water in advance. Housewives and working professionals alike have voiced their grievances, noting that the exorbitant rates charged by private water tanker operators—who have allegedly doubled their prices to exploit the sudden surge in demand—are burning a significant hole in their monthly budgets. Community leaders are now demanding a permanent, foolproof backup mechanism to ensure that Salem does not plunge into such a crisis whenever a technical fault occurs at the Mettur source.

To prevent the recurrence of such widespread water shortages in the future, civic activists and urban planners in Salem are urging the district administration to expedite the modernization of the city's aging water distribution infrastructure. They emphasize the need for installing digital flow meters and automated pressure monitoring systems that can instantly detect leaks or pressure drops, thereby minimizing water wastage and response time during pipeline bursts. Furthermore, there is a growing demand for the rejuvenation of local traditional water bodies and lakes within Salem district, which could serve as vital supplementary resources during emergency maintenance periods. Until such comprehensive infrastructural overhauls are realized, the residents of Salem remain highly vulnerable to the vagaries of a single-source supply system, highlighting the urgent need for sustainable urban water management.

According to senior transport officials, this stringent action was taken against motorists who repeatedly violated essential traffic rules, including drunk driving, speeding, carrying passengers in goods vehicles, and using mobile phones while driving. The enforcement drive was intensified following a sharp rise in road accidents across the district, prompting authorities to adopt a zero-tolerance policy against offenders. This coordinated crackdown highlights the government’s commitment to restoring order on Salem's increasingly congested roads.

The official data reveals that the suspensions were carried out between the months of April and August this year, targeting both two-wheeler riders and heavy vehicle drivers. Regional Transport Officers (RTOs) in Salem worked in close coordination with local police personnel to identify chronic offenders through systematic vehicle checks and modern surveillance cameras. Officials stated that drunk driving accounted for a significant portion of the license suspensions, followed closely by reckless driving and the failure to wear helmets or seatbelts despite repeated warnings. The authorities emphasized that suspending licenses is one of the most effective ways to deter irresponsible behavior and instill discipline among the public.

This massive enforcement drive has sent shockwaves through the local community, evoking mixed reactions from the residents of Salem. While many law-abiding citizens have welcomed the move, stating that stricter penalties are necessary to make the streets safer for pedestrians and families, others have expressed concerns over the immediate impact of these suspensions on daily commuters. Local merchants and delivery personnel, whose livelihoods depend entirely on their ability to drive, are facing severe disruptions due to the temporary loss of their driving privileges. Officials warned that any individual caught driving during their suspension period would face harsher legal consequences, including the permanent cancellation of their licenses and potential imprisonment.

Looking ahead, the Salem district administration plans to sustain this momentum by organizing extensive road safety campaigns alongside their enforcement efforts. Schools, colleges, and local community centers will be targeted to educate young drivers about the grave dangers of traffic violations and the long-term consequences of losing their driving credentials. By combining strict enforcement with public education, authorities hope to foster a culture of responsible driving across Salem. The long-anticipated air connectivity between Salem and Chennai has officially been restored, bringing immense relief to the residents and business communities of the Kongu region. The flight services, which had been temporarily suspended due to various operational challenges and low passenger turnout in the past, have now resumed under the Union Government’s regional connectivity scheme. This crucial air link was inaugurated amidst high expectations from local trade bodies, who have been relentlessly lobbying for the reinstatement of the service to foster economic growth. The resumption of this flight is expected to significantly reduce travel time between the steel city of Salem and the state capital, Chennai, which otherwise takes over six hours by road or rail.

The decision to operate the flight service comes as a strategic move to boost the industrial and commercial potential of Salem district, which is a major hub for textiles, steel, sago, and agriculture. Local entrepreneurs and corporate executives had frequently complained about the lack of rapid transport infrastructure, which hindered swift business transactions and deterred potential investors from visiting the region. By re-establishing this aerial route, the aviation authorities aim to provide a seamless travel experience for business professionals, medical tourists, and academics who frequently commute between the two cities. The flight schedule has been meticulously planned to cater to same-day travellers, allowing them to complete their work in Chennai and return to Salem by evening.

The resident welfare associations and the Salem District Chamber of Commerce have welcomed this development with great enthusiasm, noting that it will put Salem back on the aviation map of India. For several months, local activists and politicians had raised this issue at various governmental levels, emphasizing that a functional airport is vital for the overall socio-economic development of the district. Residents have expressed hope that the flight services will remain consistent this time, urging the operating airline to maintain affordable tariff structures to ensure high occupancy rates. The convenience of a one-hour flight is also expected to benefit emergency medical patients who require urgent specialized treatment at advanced healthcare facilities in Chennai.

In response to the resumption, airport authorities and local administration officials in Salem have assured that all necessary infrastructure upgrades and security protocols have been successfully put in place. The Salem Airport, located at Kamalapuram, has undergone rigorous safety audits and facility enhancements to accommodate the incoming passengers and ensure smooth operations. District officials have also stated that they are working on improving public transport connectivity and taxi services from the city centre to the airport terminal to facilitate hassle-free commuting for passengers. Furthermore, plans are reportedly underway to explore additional routes connecting Salem to other major southern cities like Bengaluru and Hyderabad in the near future, depending on the sustained success of the Chennai sector.

This renewed air connectivity is poised to act as a major catalyst for the tourism sector of the region, particularly benefiting the nearby hill station of Yercaud, which attracts thousands of visitors annually. Tour operators believe that hassle-free flight options from Chennai will encourage affluent domestic and international tourists to explore the scenic landscapes of Salem district. Additionally, the local handloom and powerloom sectors, which export unique textile products globally, will now have faster access to international cargo hubs via Chennai. As the first flights successfully take off and land at the Kamalapuram runway, there is a palpable sense of optimism that this aviation link will permanently accelerate Salem’s journey toward becoming a primary economic powerhouse in Tamil Nadu.
